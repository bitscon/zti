The gap

AI decisions are reaching production unverified.

Infrastructure changes. Automated workflows. Data operations. Most of it executes without a contract, a check, or a record.

RISK-001

Misconfigured infrastructure

AI-generated Terraform assigns public IPs to production services. No one reviewed it. It deployed.

RISK-002

Silent policy violations

AI-driven automation bypasses approval gates. Policies are defined. They are not enforced at execution.

RISK-003

No audit trail

When an AI-driven action causes an incident, there is no decision record. No lineage. No proof of what ran.

Every one of these has already happened. Probably in your stack.

The fix

Introduce a verification boundary.

ZTI sits between AI and execution. It evaluates every proposed action against a declared policy contract deterministically before anything reaches a system.

AI generates  →  ZTI verifies  →  Execution proceeds

Deterministic

Same input. Same output. Same decision. Every time.

Enforceable

Unverifiable proposals do not execute. The boundary is real.

Auditable

Every decision is sealed with a reproducible artifact and integrity hash.

ZTI does not slow down AI. It defines when AI is allowed to act.

Mechanics

Three steps. No trust required.

01

AI proposes an action

Terraform plans, workflow changes, data operations. Any AI-generated proposal that would cross a system boundary.

02

ZTI evaluates it

Policy registry lookup, constraint validation, explainability artifact generation, and integrity sealing. No inference. No probability.

03

Only verified actions execute

Proposals that pass receive a verified decision record. Everything else is logged or blocked.
